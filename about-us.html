About Us




Our Story

Years ago, the board of Christian Family Life (CFL) tasked Roland to plant a church through CFL out where he lived in North Carolina. As soon as the pandemic hit Daniel and Gio felt a tug on their heart from Tampa, Florida to explore what planting a church would look like during this crazy time. These two events, even being states apart, were happening simultaneously. Then the Llerena family moved up to North Carolina and asked Roland what it would look like to start a home church. Obviously, Roland was already on board. So, Mazevo Church began gathering. Despite, not having much to start with they knew this was what they needed to do and modeled the Acts 2 church obediently. Then 2 years after the start-up, they decided as a core team to officially plant Mazevo church in Mooresville, North Carolina.
